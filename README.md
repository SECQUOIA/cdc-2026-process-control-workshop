# Process Control in the Era of AI, Decarbonization, and Autonomy

Website for the accepted full-day workshop at the 65th IEEE Conference on Decision and Control (CDC 2026), held Monday, December 14, 2026, in Honolulu, Hawai‘i.

The workshop connects rigorous control theory, data-driven methods, and industrial practice across three themes:

- AI and learning-enabled process control
- Decarbonized industrial and energy systems
- Autonomous, integrated decision-making across timescales

## Local preview

The site is intentionally dependency-free. From the repository root, run:

```bash
python3 -m http.server 8000
```

Then open <http://localhost:8000>.

## Deployment

Pushes to `main` deploy automatically to GitHub Pages through the workflow in `.github/workflows/pages.yml`.

## Image policy

Only organizer portraits are included. Invited speakers and talk titles are not published until their participation is confirmed.
